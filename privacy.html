Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

The Oakville Guide ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by Andrew Costa, a licensed real estate salesperson with Century 21 Signature Service. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google.

Booking Requests: When you submit a viewing request, we collect the name, phone number, email address, preferred date/time, and any message you provide. This information is used solely to schedule property viewings.

Saved Listings: When you save listings to your favourites, we store your selections to sync them across your devices.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data including which screens you visit, how long you use the app, and which features you interact with. This helps us improve the app experience. This data is not shared with third parties.

Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we store your device's push notification token to send you updates about your saved listings (price changes, status changes).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and maintain the app's features, including account access, saved listings, and viewing requests

• Send push notifications about changes to your saved listings

• Respond to your booking requests and schedule property viewings

• Improve the app based on usage patterns

• Comply with legal obligations

Real Estate Listing Data

The listing data displayed in this app is provided under copyright by the Toronto Regional Real Estate Board (TRREB) through PropTX Innovations Inc. The data is deemed reliable but is not guaranteed to be accurate. Sold and closed listing data is only accessible to registered, logged-in users in compliance with TRREB's Virtual Office Website (VOW) rules.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. API tokens and secrets are stored server-side and are never exposed in the app. We use Row Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• Supabase — Authentication, database, and serverless functions

• Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method

• Resend — Email delivery for booking request notifications

• Expo — Push notification delivery

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. Booking requests are retained for business record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the personal information we hold about you

• Request correction of inaccurate information

• Request deletion of your account and data

• Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
